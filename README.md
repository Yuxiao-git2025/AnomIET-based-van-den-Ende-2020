# AnomIET-based-van-den-Ende-2020
Similar to **AnomETAS**, this method was originally used for analyzing foreshock activity anomalies in Southern California, primarily employing the **gamma** distribution of time intervals to determine the background activity rate and comparing it with a **Poisson distribution**. <br>
You may be interested in: <br>
>> van den Ende, M. P. A., & Ampuero, J.-P. (2020). On the statistical significance of foreshock sequences in Southern California. Geophysical Research Letters, 47, e2019GL086224. https://doi.org/10.1029/2019GL086224
